About Us

Welcome to Sri Durga Devi Temple, located in the divine town of Rushingi. For generations, this sacred place has served as a beacon of spirituality, attracting devotees from all over the region seeking solace and blessings.

We are dedicated to upholding the ancient traditions, celebrating major festivals with profound devotion, and providing a serene environment for meditation, prayer, and community connection.

Temples of Rushingi

Sivalayam

An ancient and serene temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its peaceful atmosphere and traditional rituals.

Ramalayalam

A vibrant temple dedicated to Lord Rama, where the community gathers for daily prayers and festive celebrations.

Sai Baba Temple

A modern and clean shrine dedicated to Shirdi Sai Baba, attracting many devotees seeking peace and blessings.
